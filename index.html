Projects

Gauloi

Hacked out in anger over a few weekends with a good friend, we did some Eth <> BTC atomic swaps. We came up with a plan to try and create a decentralised orderbook for the swaps where they would be priced as options. The goal was to sidestep the pitfalls of traditional "collateralised multisig" approaches to cross chain exchange and create a truly peer to peer ecosystem. Credit goes to 0x33a for persisting beyond reason with BDK and P2WSH.



Rust, BDK, TS.

Eth Melbourne

I helped out co-organising the Ethereum meet up in Melbourne for 2 years along with a motley crew. The meetup covers non Eth topics (Bitcoin? Farcaster?) and is more a technical meetup for blockchain developers and enthusiasts. We fielded speakers from paradigm, nethermind, railgun and others. I gave a few talks as well - one on blockchain indexers and another on the lightning network's flood and loot vuln.



eye contact, peer to peer communication
